Our Story

Founded by Abigail Afolabi with a personal mission to help others avoid the painful skin damage she experienced.

As a teenager, I used my sister's skincare product hoping it would brighten my skin. Instead, it left me with stretch marks and severe hyperpigmentation. That devastating experience became my purpose.

I became passionate about educating people on the dangers of harsh chemicals and the healing power of organic skincare. Arby's Body Organics was born from my journey—a place where nature meets knowledge, and where we prioritize skin health over quick fixes. My niche is helping people with stretch marks, dark spots, and hyperpigmentation heal naturally and safely.
